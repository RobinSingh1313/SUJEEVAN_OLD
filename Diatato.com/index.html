What is
Student Wellness ?

Student wellness refers to the overall well-being and health of students, physically,Socially,Intellectually and mentally, in an educational setting. It encompasses various aspects of a student's life, including their physical health, mental health, emotional well-being, and social relationships.

Why
Student Wellness ?

Investing in student wellness programs nurtures well-being of a student, promotes academic success, and fosters a positive educational environment. It benefits individuals, schools, and society by creating healthier and more resilient individuals who can positively contribute to their communities.
